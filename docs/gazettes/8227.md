The Communications Regulatory Authority of Namibia, in terms of regulation 34(1) of the Regulations prescribing Procedures regarding Application for, and Amendment, Renewal, Transfer and Cancellation of Spectrum Licenses, published in Government Gazette No. 6888, as General Notice No. 104, on 29 April 2019, hereby gives notice of the following decision:

The Authority resolved to withdraw spectrum license awarded to Namibian Broadcasting Corporation with effect from 30 June 2023 as set out in the Table below:

| Licensee | Radio frequencies withdrawn | Geographical coverage areas | Services provided |
|:---|:---|:---|:---|
| Namibian Broadcasting Corporation | 762 MHz | Gibeon | BROADCASTING |
| Namibian Broadcasting Corporation | 786 MHz | Shamvura | BROADCASTING |
| Namibian Broadcasting Corporation | 754 MHz | Andara | BROADCASTING |
| Namibian Broadcasting Corporation | 730 MHz | Nkurenkuru | BROADCASTING |

The full reasons for the decision can be requested from the Authority. In terms of section 31 of the Communications Act, 2009 (Act No. 8 of 2009), the Authority may, on its own motion or on a petition filed by an aggrieved party to any proceedings, reconsider any order or decision that it has made, within 90 days from the date of publication of this notice in the Gazette.
